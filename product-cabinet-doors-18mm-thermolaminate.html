Available Polytec Finishes

Gloss

A sleek, mirror-like surface that brings brightness and polish to any space. Gloss doors are easy to clean and ideal for contemporary kitchens.

Ravine

Features a textured, linear woodgrain finish that mimics the look of real timber. Ravine adds depth and natural character to cabinetry.

Smooth

A refined, low-sheen surface with a soft, uniform texture. Smooth offers a clean and versatile look suited to a range of interiors.

Texture

Provides a lightly embossed finish that gives a subtle tactile feel. Texture is durable and practical for everyday use.

Woodmatt

A matt finish with a realistic woodgrain effect, designed to look and feel like natural timber veneer. Woodmatt offers both warmth and durability.
